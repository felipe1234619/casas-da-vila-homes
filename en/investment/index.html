Commercial perspective

Beyond pricing, a matter of positioning.

Investment in Casas da Vila Homes goes beyond price per square metre. It is defined by the conjunction of location, architecture, private gardens and a scale of development that remains inherently limited. For this reason, commercial information is shared progressively, considering typology, timing of entry and the profile of the buyer.

Entry at this stage is not only about price. It is about position within a rare residential collection.
