Shared experience

The social areas are not complementary. They are part of the way of inhabiting.

At Casas da Vila Homes, the shared spaces were designed to extend the use of the residence and broaden the experience of the development. Pool, lounges, sauna and gardens structure moments of permanence that reinforce the project’s quiet and residential character.

01 · Pool

The centre of outdoor permanence

The central pool organises open-air conviviality and becomes a point of convergence between landscape, rest and everyday rhythm.

02 · Lounges

Spaces for pause and encounter

The lounges extend exterior life with a quieter language, allowing meeting, reading, rest and observation of the surroundings.

03 · Sauna

Well-being as a natural extension

The sauna does not appear as an accessory, but as an additional layer of comfort within the project’s experience of permanence.

Rhythm of use

Different moments of the day, one shared language of permanence.

Morning Pool and garden The spaces accompany a lighter and quieter use, in dialogue with vegetation and natural light.
Afternoon Lounges and encounter The shared areas absorb longer permanence, rest and conviviality without rupture with the residence.
Evening A more reserved atmosphere Lighting and spatial composition favour continuity, contemplation and a more introspective use.

Reading of the project

More than common areas, an extension of the residential experience.

Unlike developments in which shared spaces operate as compensation, here they expand the quality of permanence and reinforce the identity of the collection.
